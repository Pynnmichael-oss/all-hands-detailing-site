Booking Policies

Deposit

To hold your appointment time, bookings require a non-refundable deposit of roughly $50, applied toward your total. Deposits are due at the time of booking.

Rain Day Policy

Exterior work depends on the weather. If rain is in the forecast for your appointment window, we'll reach out ahead of time — rescheduling to the next available dry day is entirely your call, at no extra charge.

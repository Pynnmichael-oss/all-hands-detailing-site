Booking Policies

Deposit

To hold your appointment time, bookings require a non-refundable deposit of roughly $50, applied toward your total. Deposits are due at the time of booking.

Rain Day Policy

Exterior work depends on the weather. If rain is in the forecast for your appointment window, we'll reach out ahead of time — rescheduling to the next available dry day is entirely your call, at no extra charge.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Send a quote request through our contact form with your vehicle (or windows) and preferred timing, and we'll get back to you to confirm a slot. A small deposit holds your appointment — see the booking policies above.

Do you come to my home or office?

Yes — we come to your home, office, or job site anywhere in the Tulsa metro area. Prefer to drop off? We also take vehicles at 411 S Elgin Ave in downtown Tulsa.

What if I don't have access to water or electricity?

We bring professional-grade equipment and our own water, so most locations need nothing from you. If your spot has unusual constraints (a parking garage, no nearby access), mention it when booking and we'll plan around it.

How long does a typical detail take?

It depends on the vehicle's size and condition — a half detail is quicker, a full detail takes longer. We'll give you a time estimate with your quote so you can plan your day.

What's included in the membership?

Two scheduled visits every month, with vehicle alternation allowed, on a 6-month ($1,350) or 12-month ($2,700) plan — see the All Hands Membership card above.

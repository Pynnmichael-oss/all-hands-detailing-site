About All Hands Mobile Detailing

All Hands Mobile Detailing was started with a simple idea: getting your vehicle professionally cleaned shouldn't mean losing an afternoon at a shop. We bring the equipment, water, and attention to detail to you — at home, at work, or wherever your day takes you.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is straightforward: make professional-quality detailing and window cleaning something you can fit into a normal day, not something you plan a weekend around. Every visit should end with a result you'd show off to a neighbor.

We're based in the Tulsa area and built this business around treating every job — daily driver, weekend project, or the windows on your home or storefront — with the same level of care. Whether it's a quick interior refresh, a full detail, or a round of streak-free windows, we show up on time and do the work right.

That means honest quotes with no surprise add-ons, showing up in the window we promised, and standing behind the work. If something isn't right, we make it right — that's the whole "people's company" idea.

How We Got Started

All Hands began the way a lot of good local businesses do: with one person, a set of tools, and the realization that people around Tulsa wanted honest, careful work done where they actually are — at home or at work — instead of another errand across town. What started as detailing for friends and neighbors grew, job by job and referral by referral, into the mobile detailing and window cleaning service we run today.
